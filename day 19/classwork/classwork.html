Article:

football, game in which two teams of 11 players, using any part of their bodies except their hands and arms, try to maneuver the ball into the opposing team’s goal. Only the goalkeeper is permitted to handle the ball and may do so only within the penalty area surrounding the goal. The team that scores more goals wins.
Football is also stated to be one of the most, if not, the most popular sport in the world

Rules:

The aim is to score more goals than the opponent in a 90-minute playing time frame.
Each team consists of 11 players.
To score, the ball must go into the opponent’s goal.
Matches are divided into two halves of 45 minutes each, with a 15-minute half-time break.
The whole ball must cross the goal line for it to constitute a goal.
